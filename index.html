// 02 — OFFICIAL RULESET
THE RULES
By registering for any Motion event you agree to every rule below. Admin decisions are final. These apply to all formats — 2v2, 3v3, and 4v4.
REGISTRATION & ELIGIBILITY
01
Account Verification
All players must submit a Tracker.gg profile link during registration. Your account is verified by admins before the tournament. No link = no entry. No exceptions.
02
Main Accounts Only
Smurfing is an immediate permanent ban. You must compete on the exact account you registered. Alt accounts, boosted accounts, or accounts with misrepresented ranks are forbidden across all formats.
03
One Team Per Player
Players may only be on one roster per tournament. Players found on multiple rosters are removed from both teams. Rosters lock at the registration deadline — no additions after that point.
04
Substitutes
Teams may register 1 sub per team during sign-up. Sub must be registered before the tournament — no day-of additions unless an admin approves in writing before the first match.
MATCH RULES
05
Lobby Creation
The higher seeded team creates the private lobby. Password is shared via Discord DM to opponent captain only. Default: closest mutual region. Region disputes go to admin before match starts.
06
Ready-Up Window
Teams have 5 minutes after their match is called to be in the lobby. After 5 minutes, the opposing team may request a forfeit win. This is strictly enforced. Be ready before your match is called.
07
Substitutions Mid-Series
Subs may only swap between games in a series — never during a live game. For 4v4: all 4 players must be registered. No mid-series swaps in 4v4 under any circumstance.
08
Disconnections
If a player disconnects before a goal is scored, both teams may agree to replay that game. If a goal was already scored, the game continues. Full lobby crashes are reviewed by admin only.
SCORE REPORTING & DISPUTES
09
Reporting Results
Winning team captain posts the final score + screenshot in #match-results within 5 minutes of the series ending. Losing captain confirms with ✅. Admin updates bracket.
10
Disputes
Disputes must be raised in #disputes within 10 minutes of result. Evidence required from both teams. Admin reviews and rules — decision is final and immediate. No further appeals.
CONDUCT
11
Sportsmanship
Motion expects GGs. Toxic behavior, harassment, slurs, or targeted abuse toward any player, admin, or spectator — in-game, Discord, or publicly — results in immediate disqualification.
12
Intentional Griefing
Intentional self-destruction, AFK play, or any deliberate attempt to throw a match is treated as a conduct violation. Admins may review replay footage. Confirmed griefers are disqualified.
13
Repeat Violations
First major violation = disqualification from current event. Repeated violations = permanent server ban. Motion is a community — protect it. Admins reserve the right to ban at any time for conduct deemed harmful to the community.
14
Admin Authority
All admin decisions are final. Continued public complaints after a final ruling may result in disciplinary action. Admins are volunteers — respect them. Contact an admin privately for any serious concerns.
